❓ Common Questions

What happens after 3 months?
Your class expires and student submission data is cleared from the database. Students keep their personal app access forever. You simply renew to start a new class anytime.
How do students join my class?
After purchasing, you receive a unique class code. Share it with your students. They log into the app, enter your class code, and they are instantly enrolled in your class.
Do students need to pay separately?
Yes. Each student purchases the $7 student plan which gives them full app access forever. The teacher plan covers the class management and submission features.
How do I receive payments?
All payments are processed securely through Stripe and deposited directly into your bank account within 2 business days.
Can I upgrade my plan?
Yes. If your class grows you can upgrade your plan at any time and the difference will be applied to your account.
