Instructions

Follow the guidelines below to complete each chapter.

Step 1 – Read the title on the chapter page.

Step 2 – Memorize the theme listed for each passage on the chapter page.

Lesson #1 — Scripture Page

Step 3 – Read each passage printed.

Step 4 – Memorize the theme listed.

Step 5 – Read and highlight any words, phrases, or themes from the passage that interest you.

Treasure Chest Page

Step 6 – Place your highlighted notes from the scripture page into your treasure chest.

Step 7 – Memorize the theme listed.

Question Page

Step 8 – Read the passage printed again.

Step 9 – Memorize the theme listed.

Step 10 – Answer the listed questions.

Imagination Sketch Page

Step 11 – Read the listed passage printed again.

Step 12 – Memorize the theme listed.

Step 13 – Use your imagination to sketch or write your insight from the passage.

Lesson #2 — Repeat Steps 1–13

Review Page

Step 14 – List the two main themes.

Step 15 – List the scriptures that support the two main themes.

Personal Application Page – Ask God for understanding and list the life application(s) as the Holy Spirit reveals them.
